additional information


purpose


                    research and development for data analysis applications, including data modeling and algorithm development and application.
                



essential functions


                    
                        design, develop, configure, apply, and test data analysis algorithms. prepare technical documentation and technical presentations. communicate with project team members and supervisors, and sponsors for timely implementation of project.
                    
                



marginal/incidental functions


                    
                        other related functions as assigned.
                    
                



required qualifications


                    
                        bachelor's degree plus three years of work experience in computer science, engineering, math, or other applied sciences.  experience working with data analysis and data mining algorithms (machine learning, regression, probability, and statistics).  strong math background and technical writing skills.  demonstrated experience programming in java or python.  strong analytical and problem solving capability.  ability to work independently with sensitive and confidential information, maintain a professional demeanor, work as a team member without daily supervision, and effectively communicate with diverse groups. u.s. citizen. applicant selected will be subject to a government security investigation and must meet eligibility requirements for access to classified information at the level appropriate to the project requirements of the position.
                    
                



preferred qualifications


                    master's degree in a required area.  advanced coursework or significant experience related to data analysis and artificial intelligence (statistics, data mining, scalable machine learning, pattern recognition, etc.).  experience with  r, sas, stata, mathematica, and/or matlab.  experience with database integration, sql programming, and/or data mining and data exploration tools, methods, and infrastructure (hadoop, hbase, r, spark, solr, elastic search, etc.).   experience with software version control systems.  experience analyzing large, complex datasets using scalable techniques.  experience working in the cyber security application domain. cumulative gpa of 3.0.
